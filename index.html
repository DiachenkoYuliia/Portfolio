UnivArena

Project Overview

A university esports tournament organized for students, supported by FEDET, featuring a League of Legends competition with both on-site matches and a Twitch livestream. The project aimed to bring students together around digital culture, build an engaged community, and increase event visibility through strategic communication and visual content.

Challenge

Launching a new university esports event required building awareness from scratch and attracting a large student audience in a competitive digital environment. The main objective was to reach 200 participants, create excitement around the League of Legends tournament, and establish a consistent communication strategy across multiple platforms.

My Role

As Digital Communication Manager, I developed the communication strategy, managed digital promotion, and created visual content to support the tournament's growth.

Process

Built the event's visual identity and communication strategy by creating Instagram content (posts and stories), promotional posters, roll-up banners, and a teaser video distributed across university campuses in two cities.

Final Outcome

The campaign exceeded the initial goal, achieving 450+ registrations and successfully establishing a strong digital identity for a new university esports initiative. The project successfully combined digital and offline communication, creating a recognizable visual identity across social media, campus materials, and the Twitch livestream experience.

Breathing Academy

Project Overview

Breathing Academy is a French institute specializing in functional breathing education, offering professional trainings, courses, workshops, and immersive experiences.

Challenge

The project focused on strengthening the Academy's digital communication, maintaining consistent branding, and promoting its educational programs through websites, social media, email campaigns, and offline materials.

My Role

As Digital Communications Manager & Web Content Assistant, I created visual content and supported website management. I developed content strategies, updated two WordPress websites, produced photo and video content, and designed digital and print materials.

Process

Created social media content including posts, Stories, and Reels; produced and edited photo/video materials; updated website content; designed newsletters via Mailjet; and developed promotional assets such as flyers, brochures, roll-up banners, and other printed materials.

Final Outcome

Strengthened Breathing Academy's social media presence by developing a promotion strategy, introducing new content formats and recurring rubrics, and creating consistent visual communication. The project improved the brand's positioning, increased online visibility, and helped build a stronger connection with the audience across digital channels.

Radio Campus Orléans

Project Overview

Radio Campus Orléans is an independent radio station with a large community of presenters, broadcasting continuously and actively participating in cultural events, festivals, and local initiatives. The project involved supporting the station's media production and digital communication through content creation and event coverage.

Challenge

The challenge was to capture the energy of live events and transform it into engaging digital content while supporting the station's communication needs and maintaining a strong connection with its audience and cultural partners.

My Role

As a Digital Content Creator, I was responsible for producing multimedia content and supporting the station's digital communication. I created photo and video content, developed social media materials, wrote website articles, and managed communication with artists during interviews and events.

Process

Captured and edited photo/video content during events, created social media materials, and wrote website articles to highlight the station's activities. Coordinated with artists during interviews and supported radio productions through technical preparation and event setup.

Final Outcome

Enhanced Radio Campus Orléans' digital communication by creating engaging multimedia content, showcasing cultural events, and strengthening the connection between the radio station, artists, and its audience.

Luna Rossa

Project Overview

Luna Rossa Production is an entertainment company that provides professional entertainers and performers for a wide range of private and corporate events. Through themed performances, interactive activities, and live entertainment, the company creates engaging and memorable experiences for its audiences.

Challenge

The goal was to strengthen the company’s digital presence, showcase its services more effectively, and develop a consistent visual identity that reflected the creativity and energy of its events.

My Role

As a Social Media & Content Specialist, I managed Luna Rossa Production’s digital communication across Instagram and TikTok. My responsibilities included content creation, visual production, video editing, publishing, and social media management, with a focus on increasing brand visibility and audience engagement.

Process

I produced and edited photo and video content, managed Instagram and TikTok publications, and designed promotional assets, including banners and campaign visuals. I adapted each piece of content to the specific requirements of each platform while maintaining a consistent brand identity. I also supported the promotion of the company’s services through organic content and targeted Facebook Ads and TikTok Ads campaigns.

Final Outcome

The project strengthened Luna Rossa Production’s online presence through more consistent social media communication and a clearer visual identity. The combination of organic content and targeted advertising increased the visibility of its services and presented the brand’s creative universe more effectively across digital platforms.

Poster Design

Project Overview

A graphic design project created during a design course at Projector Institute in Ukraine, focused on exploring the relationship between visual communication and urban culture. The task was to transform poetry and song lyrics into a series of posters designed for public spaces in Kyiv.

Challenge

The challenge was to translate abstract ideas, emotions, and literary references into strong visual concepts that could communicate with an urban audience. The goal was to create posters that were not only visually appealing but also meaningful, recognizable, and suitable for both print and digital formats.

My Role

As a graphic design student, I developed a complete poster series from concept development to final production. I worked on visual research, sketches, composition, typography, and illustration while applying design principles learned with a mentor.

Process

The project followed a complete design workflow, starting with concept development and visual exploration, followed by sketches, composition experiments, and digital execution in Adobe Illustrator. I prepared the final artworks for both printed materials and digital use, considering technical requirements for different formats.

Final Outcome

Created a series of 10 original posters combining poetry, music, and visual storytelling. The project strengthened my skills in conceptual design, typography, illustration, and preparing graphic materials for real-world applications in urban environments.
